# MotorRelay Product Roadmap

MotorRelay should become a trusted vehicle movement marketplace, not just a jobs board.

## Core Workflow

1. Dealer posts a vehicle movement.
2. Vetted drivers request the job.
3. Dealer assigns the best driver.
4. Driver collects and delivers the vehicle.
5. Driver uploads delivery proof.
6. Dealer approves completion.
7. Invoice is generated and payment is released.

## Standout Features

- Driver verification: licence, identity, selfie, insurance checks, admin approval.
- Dealer verification: trade policy, trade plates, company details, billing status.
- Job matching: distance, availability, driver rating, transport type, route suitability.
- Mobile driver flow: accept, navigate, share location, upload proof, mark delivered.
- Evidence trail: timestamps, messages, GPS, photos, signed PDFs, invoice history.
- Dispute handling: admin review, refund notes, payout hold, account suspension.
- Notifications: push, email, SMS, and in-app alerts for all workflow changes.

## Revenue Model

- Platform commission on completed jobs.
- Urgent boost fees for dealers.
- Dealer subscriptions for higher job limits, team seats, analytics, and priority support.
- Driver premium tools for planner access, wider job radius, and faster payouts.
- Enterprise accounts for large dealer groups and logistics partners.

## Payment Roadmap

- Add payment provider integration.
- Take dealer payment before or at assignment.
- Hold funds until delivery proof is approved.
- Release driver payout after approval and fraud checks.
- Keep platform fee automatically.
- Support refunds, disputes, and payout holds.

## Mobile Roadmap

- Use Capacitor first for Android testing.
- Add push notifications.
- Add camera/photo upload for delivery proof.
- Add geolocation tracking with clear driver consent.
- Add offline-safe proof capture for poor signal areas.

## Admin Roadmap

- Verify drivers and dealers.
- Monitor stuck jobs and late deliveries.
- Review disputes and refund requests.
- Track revenue, subscriptions, fees, and payout holds.
- Suspend risky accounts.
